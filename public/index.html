About

Hi, I'm Alexey

I'm a senior software engineer in Munich. I build web apps, microservices, and the cloud infrastructure they run on, across IoT, fintech, and public-sector systems.

I like owning things end to end and working on small teams where I get to touch everything.

I work mostly in TypeScript, Go, and Python these days, with deep roots in Java and Kotlin, and I lean on AI-assisted development to move faster. But technologies and tools are just instruments. What they're really for is building something for people that lights up their day - the way an aurora lights up the dark.
