About

Hi, I'm Alexey.

I'm a software engineer with 12 years of experience building reliable, user-friendly applications. I enjoy releasing new features and connecting with the people who use them — seeing how the work actually makes a difference is the best part of the job.

Day to day I work mostly in Kotlin, Java, TypeScript, and on AWS. I've built everything from payment systems and OAuth 2.0 servers to voice-assistant integrations and Flutter apps for field installers.

Outside of work I play in a rock band called Quantum Fate, co-built a small game called ssgg.fun, and spend a lot of time playing computer games with friends and family.
